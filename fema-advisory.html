FEMA Advisory & Compliance

Simplifying Cross-Border Transactions

We provide comprehensive FEMA (Foreign Exchange Management Act) and RBI compliance services to businesses engaged in international transactions. Our services ensure smooth execution of foreign investments, remittances, and regulatory filings while maintaining full compliance with RBI and FEMA regulations.

Whether you are an Indian company receiving foreign investment or expanding overseas, we provide end-to-end FEMA advisory and compliance support.

01

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Compliance

We assist companies & individuals in receiving and managing foreign investments in compliance with FEMA regulations.

FDI Advisory under Automatic & Approval Route
Share Allotment Compliance for Foreign Investors
Filing of FC-GPR for Issue of Shares to Non-Residents
Filing of FC-TRS for Transfer of Shares
Valuation Certification as per FEMA Guidelines
Issue of Share Certificates & Post-Investment Compliance
RBI Reporting & Documentation Support
02

Overseas Direct Investment (ODI) Services

We help Indian entities invest and expand globally with full regulatory compliance.

Advisory on Overseas Direct Investment (ODI)
Filing of Form ODI (including ODI–Part I)
Obtaining UIN (Unique Identification Number)
Annual Performance Report (APR) Filing
Restructuring of Overseas Investments
ODI Closure & Disinvestment Compliance

External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) Compliance

Advisory on ECB Eligibility & Structure
ECB Reporting & Compliance
Filing of ECB Returns
Loan Agreement Documentation Review
End-use Monitoring Compliance

Buyback of Shares from Non-Residents
